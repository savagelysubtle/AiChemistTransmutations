# Privacy Policy for AiChemist Transmutation Codex

**Last Updated:** October 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AiChemist Transmutation Codex ("we", "our", or "the Application") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our document conversion software.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **License Information**: When you purchase and activate a license, we collect your license key and activation information for validation purposes
- **Support Requests**: If you contact support, we may collect your email address and the content of your communications

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Telemetry** (with your consent):
  - Feature usage statistics (which converters you use)
  - Conversion success/failure rates
  - Performance metrics (conversion times, file sizes - not file contents)
  - Error types and frequency
  - Application version and operating system information
  - Anonymous session identifiers

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly **do not** collect:

- File contents or document data
- File names or file paths
- Personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses
- Browsing history or behavior outside the Application
- Device identifiers or location data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **License Validation**: Verifying your license status and entitlements
2. **Product Improvement**: Understanding feature usage to prioritize development
3. **Bug Detection**: Identifying and fixing errors and performance issues
4. **Analytics**: Understanding aggregate usage patterns (never individual behavior)

## Telemetry and Consent

- Telemetry is **completely optional** and requires explicit opt-in consent
- You can grant or revoke consent at any time through the Application settings
- All telemetry data is anonymous and cannot be linked back to individual users
- We use telemetry data only in aggregate to improve the Application
- No telemetry data is sold or shared with third parties

## Data Storage and Security

### License Data

- License validation data is stored on our secure servers hosted by Supabase
- All connections use HTTPS encryption
- License keys are validated using cryptographic signatures

### Telemetry Data

- Telemetry events (if you opt-in) are stored securely using Supabase
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel only

### Local Storage

- License status and telemetry consent preferences are stored locally on your device
- Application logs are stored locally in platform-specific directories:
  - Windows: `%APPDATA%/AiChemist/logs`
  - macOS: `~/Library/Application Support/AiChemist/logs`
  - Linux: `~/.local/share/aichemist/logs`

## Data Retention

- **License Data**: Retained for the duration of your license plus 1 year for support purposes
- **Telemetry Data**: Retained for up to 2 years for analysis purposes
- **Local Data**: You can delete local data by uninstalling the Application or manually deleting the data directories

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

1. **Supabase** (Database and Backend):
   - Purpose: License validation and telemetry storage
   - Data shared: License keys, usage telemetry (if consented)
   - Privacy Policy: <https://supabase.com/privacy>

2. **Gumroad** (Payment Processing):
   - Purpose: License purchases
   - Data shared: Payment information (handled entirely by Gumroad)
   - Privacy Policy: <https://gumroad.com/privacy>

We do not control these third-party services and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

## Your Rights

### Access and Control

- **View License Status**: Check your license status in the Application
- **Manage Telemetry**: Enable or disable telemetry at any time
- **Data Deletion**: Request deletion of your license and telemetry data

### GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: <privacy@aichemist.app>

The Application is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
